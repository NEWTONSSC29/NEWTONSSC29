Museum Exploring

I’m someone who appreciates both the energy of the court and the quiet of a gallery. Aside from playing basketball, I love spending my free time wandering through museums. There’s a unique kind of inspiration I get from studying a masterpiece that’s totally different from the adrenaline of a game, and I enjoy the shift in pace.

Playing Guitar & Singing

In my free time, I’m often found with a guitar in hand. Whether I’m experimenting with melodies or finally mastering a song I've loved for years, playing is my creative outlet. There’s something uniquely grounding about the tactile feel of the strings and the way a good session clears my mind.
