Taal Volcano: For a long time, Taal was famous for being an island (Vulcan Point) inside a lake (Main Crater Lake) inside an island (Volcano Island) inside a lake (Taal Lake) on an island (Luzon).

Note: Recent eruptions in 2020 significantly altered the landscape, drying up much of the inner crater lake.

Star City: As one of the country's most iconic all-weather amusement parks, Star City is home to "Snow World," the largest permanent ice attraction in the Philippines that maintains a freezing -15°C year-round. It also houses the headquarters of the Manila Broadcasting Company, meaning many of the nation’s popular radio stations are broadcast live from right inside the park.
