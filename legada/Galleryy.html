Fourth Picture: Taoist temple

Me and my family went to Cebu, and we planned on going to a Taoist temple. When we got inside, we were in awe seeing the colorful dragons and murals. Their practices were very intresting to us. In a sanctuary, there was this way to tell people's fortunes, it was the use of stones, and whatever position the stones can foretell your fortune in the future.

DEFINITIONS:

Taosim
-is an ancient Chinese philosophical and religious tradition centered on living in harmony with the Tao, or "the Way," which is considered the fundamental, underlying principle of the universe.
Mural
-any piece of graphic artwork that is painted or applied directly to a wall, ceiling or other permanent substrate.
Dragon (Chinese folklore)
-dragons (Long) are benevolent, powerful, and wise beings that symbolize imperial power, good fortune, and control over weather, particularly rain.
