I have several hobbies that I enjoy in my free time. These activities keep me happy and energized.

1. Dance

I love to dance because it helps me express myself. It's fun, a great exercise, and I get to meet new people.

Here's a quote I like:

"If you are on stage, dance like you own the stage."

My favorite type of dance on YouTube.

2. Hanging Out with Family and Friends

Spending time with loved ones brings me joy and helps me relax. We often share stories, laugh, and make great memories together.

3. Painting

Painting allows me to turn ideas into colorful art. It's peaceful and encourages creativity and focus.

4. Reading Books

Books open up new worlds. I especially enjoy romance and fantasy. One of my favorite books is Powerless.

Here’s a quote from a book I love:

“And I’ll save your life again and again, aimlessly hoping you will allow me to stay in it.”

5. Listening to Music

Music helps me relax and feel inspired. I enjoy pop the most.

My favorite pop artists include Taylor Swift13 and BTSARMY — their music always lifts my mood.
